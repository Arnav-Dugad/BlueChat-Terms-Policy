Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

BlueChat collects minimal data necessary to provide our services:

Account Information

When you register, we collect your phone number to verify your identity and create your account.

Contacts

With your permission, we access your phone's contact list to help you find friends who are already using BlueChat. We do not store your contacts on our servers permanently; they are used only for matching purposes.

Messages

BlueChat uses end-to-end encryption where possible. Your messages are stored on your device. Messages are only stored on our servers temporarily while they are being delivered to the recipient. Once delivered, they are deleted from our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To connect you with other users.
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • We never sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
